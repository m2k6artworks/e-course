Course Detail

In this course, you will master the essential techniques for transforming grayscale images into vibrant, colorful character designs using Krita. Starting with silhouette creation and Lambert lighting principles, you will progress to grayscale painting, adding depth and dimension to your characters. Finally, you'll learn to bring your artwork to life with rich colors and detailed painting techniques. This comprehensive course is perfect for aspiring digital artists looking to enhance their character design skills.
