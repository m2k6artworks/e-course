Course Detail

In this course, you will learn the fundamentals of Agile and Waterfall project management, starting from understanding the Agile Manifesto to adopting an Agile mindset. You will be introduced to the popular Scrum methodology and other widely used Agile methods. Additionally, the course will cover blending methods that can be applied to enhance flexibility and effectiveness in project management. By taking this course, you will gain a comprehensive understanding of how to manage projects in a more adaptive and responsive manner to changes.

What will you learn?

Agile & Waterfall Project Management
Agile Manifesto
Agile Mindset
Introduction to Scrum
Other Popular Agile Methodologies
